The Shark Tank

Over the years many startup companies have found the initial investments they needed to scale with the help of the “sharks,” who are ready to invest their money for a small stake in the company. The data used for the following interactive visualizations give a variety of information related to pitches that have been presented over the first six seasons of Shark Tank. This dashboard page provides charts, maps, and plots that our users can filter and manipulate to gain insight about the categories, locations, valuations, and sharks that were a part of the deals made and deals DENIED!
